About Me

I am a health care policy wonk learning software development who just moved to the Philadelphia area from Nashville, Tennessee. I've been all over the place: born in California, mostly grew up in Atlanta, went to ​college​ in rural Ohio​, grad school​ Athens, Georgia, and served two years in the Peace Corps in Cameroon.​

I've become interested in computer programming the last couple of years​. I've completed the Ruby on Rails course through Bloc, a mentor-led online coding bootcamp. I also dabble with data and visualizations using R. I hope to learn more about JavaScript and Python in the future.

I've also been building my personal web site - this one right here​ - ​from scratch​, with a little help from HTML & CSS: Design and Build Websites, my Bloc classes, and every programmer's favorite resources: The Google and Stack Overflow.

In addition to computer-y stuff, I love the NBA, watching The Good Wife with my fiancée, walking our dog, and trying not to get bit by the cat.
